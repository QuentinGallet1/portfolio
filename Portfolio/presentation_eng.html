Atos presentation

Atos is one of the biggest DSC (Digital Service Company) in the world, it was founded in 1997 by Bernard Bourigeaud. It is thus a french company and his head office is located in Bezons, France.

As every other DSC, Atos is specialized in outsourcing, information systems management and consulting.

The company has a strong impact in Europe where he is leader in cloud services, cybersecurity and supercomputing. But despite all of that, Atos is everywhere across the globe (located in more than 72 countries) on almost all continents.

It benefits of many parterships with big companies such as AWS(amazon), Google or even IBM.

Very aware about nowadays challenges about climate changes, Atos does whatever it takes to decarbonize his data centers and supports the Green IT (green informatic)

In total, Atos counts about 110 000 employes and had a turnover of 10 billions euros in 2021, making it one of the biggest informatic companies.
